# ⚖️ Legal Document Simplifier

A web-based tool that transforms complex legal language into clear, easy-to-understand text.

## 📋 Overview

Legal Document Simplifier is designed to help users quickly understand legal documents by converting complicated legal jargon and complex sentence structures into plain, accessible language. This tool saves time and reduces the need for specialized legal expertise to comprehend important legal documents.

## 🎯 Key Features

- **Document Simplification**: Converts complex legal language into everyday language
- **Time-Saving**: Quickly understand key terms and conditions
- **Accessibility**: Makes legal documents accessible to non-lawyers
- **Easy-to-Use Interface**: Simple web-based tool requiring no installation
